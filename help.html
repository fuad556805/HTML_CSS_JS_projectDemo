🚀 Getting Started

When you first open StudyHub, you'll be asked to enter your name. This personalizes your dashboard with a greeting. You can always change it by clicking "✏️ Edit Name" on the home page.

💡 Tip: All your data (tasks, notes, events) is saved automatically in your browser. Clearing browser data will erase it.
  • Click the ☰ hamburger icon (top-left) to open the sidebar menu
  • Click anywhere outside the sidebar or press Esc to close it
  • The Home dashboard shows quick stats for all your tools

🔢 Calculator

  • Switch between Basic and Scientific mode using the tabs
  • Scientific mode adds: sin, cos, tan, log, ln, √, |x|, x², xⁿ, π, e, 1/x
  • Memory: M+ stores current result, MR recalls it, MC clears
  • Full keyboard support — type numbers and operators directly
  • Press Enter or = to calculate, Esc to clear all

✅ To-Do List

  • Type a task and press Enter or click + Add
  • Choose a category (Study, Personal, Work, Health) and priority
  • Optionally set a due date — overdue tasks are highlighted in red
  • Click the circle to mark done; click ✏️ to edit; 🗑️ to delete

📝 Notes

  • Click + New to create a note
  • Use the formatting toolbar for Bold, Italic, Underline, and lists
  • Notes auto-save every 2 seconds while you type
  • Press Ctrl+S to save manually
  • Click ⬇️ Download to save the note as a .txt file

⏱️ Stopwatch & Timer

  • Stopwatch tab: Start, pause, resume. Click 🏁 Lap to record lap times
  • Best lap is shown in green, slowest in red
  • Countdown tab: Set hours, minutes, seconds and start

⚖️ BMI Calculator

  • Choose Metric (cm/kg) or Imperial (ft+in/lbs)
  • Enter height, weight, age, and gender for detailed results
  • The visual gauge shows your position in the BMI scale

🔄 Unit Converter

  • Select a category from the pills at the top
  • Choose "from" and "to" units, then type a value
  • Results update instantly as you type
  • Click ⇄ Swap to reverse the conversion

📅 Calendar

  • Click any day to select it and see its events
  • Use ‹ › arrows to navigate months
  • Fill in the event form and click Add Event
  • Click an event to delete it

🎯 Guess the Number

  • Choose a range: 1–50, 1–100, or 1–200
  • Type your guess and click Guess! or press Enter
  • The game tells you if the number is Too High or Too Low
  • Hints narrow down the range as you guess
  • Your wins and best score are saved automatically

💬 Frequently Asked Questions

Will my data be lost if I close the browser? ▼
No! All data is saved in your browser's localStorage, which persists even when you close and reopen the browser. However, clearing browser data/cache will erase it.
Does StudyHub work offline? ▼
Yes! Once the page is loaded, all features work without an internet connection.
Can I use it on my phone? ▼
Absolutely! StudyHub is fully responsive and works great on mobile phones and tablets.
How do I change my name? ▼
On the Home page, click the "✏️ Edit Name" button in the welcome banner.
Can I export my notes? ▼
Yes! Open any note and click the "⬇️ Download" button to save it as a .txt file.
